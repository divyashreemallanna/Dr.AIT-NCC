2 KARNATAKA BATTALION NCC

National Cadet Corps is a youth organization in India that aims to develop character, discipline, leadership, and a spirit of adventure among young citizens.
Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology comes under 2 Karnataka Battalion NCC which is a distinguished unit within the NCC structure. It falls under the jurisdiction of the Karnataka and Goa Directorate NCC, Bangalore 'A' Group. This is one of the battalion responsible for coordinating and executing NCC activities and training programs in the Karnataka region. It operates under the guidance of the NCC Directorate and is instrumental in nurturing the youth of Karnataka, instilling in them the values and skills necessary to become responsible and patriotic citizens.
The NCC, through units like the 2 Karnataka Battalion NCC, continues to empower students of Dr.AIT and other colleges with a sense of duty towards the country, while also fostering personal growth and development. It promotes a holistic approach to education by complementing academic learning with practical experiences that help in shaping well-rounded individuals who are not only academically proficient but also socially aware and responsible
